Sacred Pujas & Homas Offered

Select from our list of traditional pujas. Custom rituals can be organized according to your regional tradition and community needs.

Temple Consecration

Kumbhabishekam

A highly sacred temple consecration ceremony performed to purify, energize, and dedicate the temple structure and deities according to strict Agama scriptures.

New Beginnings

Griha Pravesh Puja

A powerful ceremony designed to clear negative vibrations in a new house and invoke protective deities to ensure a happy stay.

Prosperity & Blessings

Satyanarayana Puja

Seek blessings of Lord Vishnu for health, peace, and abundance. Usually performed on Full Moon days (Purnima) or special family milestones.

Obstacle Removal

Ganapathi Homa

Invoke Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles, to bestow success, intelligence, and fortune before embarking on new business or personal goals.

Divine Protection & Victory

Shudarshana Aradhana & Homa

Perform the sacred worship and fire ritual (Homa) dedicated to Lord Sudarshana to remove negative energies, evil eyes, health issues, and invoke prosperity, success, and divine peace.

Divine Deity Weddings

Devara Kalyana & Unions

A grand and spiritually uplifting Kalyanotsavam (sacred wedding of deities) performed with traditional setup, decoration, and precise chanting of Vedic mantras to bring prosperity, peace, and harmony.

Frequently Asked Questions

Clear your doubts regarding booking, priest allocations, puja materials, and regional traditions.

What locations do you serve?
We primarily conduct pujas in homes, offices, and temples across Bangalore, Chennai, and Hyderabad. We can also travel for special family events or large temple installations upon request.
Do you bring the puja samagri (materials)?
Yes! We offer a full-package service which includes all the high-quality, pure, and organic puja materials. We will take care of the flowers and fruits. You only need to arrange simple household items.
In which languages can you perform the rituals?
Our priests are fluent in Vedic Sanskrit and can perform rituals with regional explanations in Kannada, Telugu, Tamil, Malayalam, Hindi, and English based on your preferences.
How do I pay for the booking?
You can pay a small booking advance online (UPI, Netbanking, or Card) and pay the remaining balance to the priest directly in cash or UPI after the successful completion of the puja.
